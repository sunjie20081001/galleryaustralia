About us

Gallery Australia Pty Ltd

Gallery Australia Pty Ltd , established in Sydney in 2016, is an integrated exporting company scoping to source and market various of high standard agricultural products from Australia and New Zealand, including beef, lamb, wheat, barley, sorghum, mung beans, peas, other grain cereals and food products (dairy products, bakery, manuka honey, cherry, alcoholic beverages, etc.) with a large volume. We are also laying out industrial investments in related fields at the right time.
